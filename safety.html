Safety

Civil Placement is dedicated to the safety of its clients and their staff

We strive to ensure that our clients are able to create and maintain safe worksites where injuries do not occur. By providing a secure worksite for their employees, companies are able to maintain productivity and workplace continuity by minimising disruptions.

Our procedures and operations are guided by strict safety guidelines to ensure that Civil Placement and its clients are able to work in safety and security at all times

Civil Placement has a proud track record of safety, including:

  • No fatalities
  • No prosecutions or investigations
  • No environmental incidents.
